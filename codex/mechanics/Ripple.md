
# Concept

Ripple represents waves of magical tension that gets release when particular corners of the Rupture are accessed by the mage. They spread trough reality and have great damaging impact on all the spells. Great mastery is required to [[Spell#Weave|Weave]] spells resilient to these waves.

Ripple is triggered by player taking [[Runes]] from designated spaces on the [[Rupture Board]]. When [[Rune Tiles|Rune Tile]] is taken from the designated Ripple space, resolve the Ripple event before any other steps.

# Resolving Ripple

Do the steps if following order:
- Draw [[Rune Tiles|Rune Tile]] from the [[Rune Tile Bag]] and note it's [[Runes|Rune]] and [[Rune Tiles#Polarity|Polarity]].
- All players in turn order finds ALL [[Rune Tiles]] in their current [[Spell]] that match the [[Runes|Rune]] and [[Rune Tiles|Polarity]] of the selected [[Rune Tiles|Rune Tile]] and processes their [[Spell#Collapse|Collapse]].
- If any [[Rune Tiles]] becomes disconnected by the collapse of the affected tiles, [[Spell#Collapse|Collapse]] the chosen spell parts until all the spells adhere to the rules. (Discarding [[Incantations]] for each normally ...)
- Return the drawn tile to the [[Rune Tile Bag]].
