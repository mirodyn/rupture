# Concept

*Realignment is a phase of the game that represents reality adjusting to the rupture. The conditions change, new possibilities arise others are lost. Realignment is caused by too many [[Ripple|Ripples]] during the [[Spell#Weaving|Weaving]] of the spells.* 
In the game terms, realignment is basically upkeep phase that progresses the game further and can end the game. Its the only time when potential death of player is checked.
The phase is largely automated with minimal player decisions, but some [[Incantations]], [[Domains]] and [[Resonance|Resonances]] can provided players with actions and decisions as well. The phase is divided into following steps in this fixed order: 

- [[Realignment#Tension Step|Tension]]
- [[Realignment#Instability Step|Instability]]
- [[Realignment#Incantations Refresh Step|Incantations Refresh]]
- [[Realignment#Survival Step|Survival]]
- [[Realignment#Game End Step|Game End]]
- [[Realignment#Refresh Step|Refresh]]

After all of the steps are done, start new [[Game structure#Weaving|Weaving]] phase.
# Tension Step

*If the tension of the rupture substrate is constantly raising. If not relieved in time, contraction happens. Nobody should be in peace with that ...*

Look at the [[Initiative - Tension token]], if its facing the [[Initiative - Tension token#Tension side|Tension Side]] up, start the [[Realignment#Contraction Event|Contraction Event]]. Otherwise, if there are any [[Shard Tokens]] in the [[Rupture Board#Calm Zone|Calm zone]] of the Rupture board, start the [[Realignment#Crack Event|Crack Event]]. If none of those happen, continue with next step. 
## Contraction Event

Starting with the player holding [[Initiative - Tension token]] everyone chooses one domain, [[Resonance#Acquiring Resonance|Acquires Resonance]] of said domain and then, removes one of the [[Shard Tokens]] from the [[Domain Boards|Domain Board]]. The same Domain cannot be chosen twice. **Players will not keep this [[Shard Tokens]], instead, these tokens are removed from the game.**

## Crack Event

Crack Event is caused by [[Shard Tokens]] left in the [[Rupture Board#Calm Zone|Calm zone]], most commonly by eliminated players.

- Starting with the player holding [[Initiative - Tension token]] everyone chooses one domain corresponding with one of the [[Shard Tokens]] present in the [[Rupture Board#Calm Zone|Calm zone]]. Same Domain cannot be chosen twice. If there are not enough different [[Shard Tokens]] for every player to choose from, the players without any available option won't choose any and these players won't participate in the next step. 
 - All players that have chosen Domain in previous step will [[Resonance#Acquiring Resonance|Acquires Resonance]] of said domain and then, removes one of the [[Shard Tokens]] from the [[Domain Boards|Domain Board]]. **Players will not keep this [[Shard Tokens]], instead, these tokens are removed from the game.**
# Instability Step

*Magical domains are powerful but fragile in nature. Draining them of their power makes them volatile. But they remember.*

Take a look at all [[Domain Boards]], if any has all of its [[Shard Tokens]] removed from the board and does not have [[Unstable marker]] put on it yet, trigger its [[Realignment#Instability Event|Instability Event]]. 
If multiple such events would get triggered at the same time, player holding [[Initiative - Tension token]] decides the order.
## Instability Event

- Every player with [[Resonance Cards]] in their [[Game Layout#Active zone|Active zone]] flips all [[Resonance Cards]] belonging to the unstable [[Domains|Domain]].
- Put [[Unstable marker]] on the [[Domain Boards|Domain board]] of affected domain.
# Incantations Refresh Step
*Reality does not play according our rules. Not everything happens in the percieved order. It requires great  judgment to predicts what is and what is not to come.*

- All players discard all of their [[Incantation Cards]] without [[Focus Tokens]] placed on them form the [[Game Layout#Active zone|Active zone]]. 
- All players shuffle their Status decks.
- All players draw new [[Incantation Cards]] from their Status deck. Draw 1 card + 1 additional card for every active [[Realignment#Draw Icon|Draw Icon]]. If [[Incantation Cards]] with [[Realignment#Draw Icon|Draw Icon]] were drawn during this step, those icons are immediately active and have to be resolved as well. Continue drawing this way while you have cards to draw.

### Draw Icon 
Valid Draw Icon is such that is visible in [[Game Layout#Active zone|Active zone]] or on the [[Domain Boards]].
# Survival Step
*Time ticks. Tick, tock ...*

Each player count their total amount of [[Realignment#Hearths and Skulls|Hearths and Skulls]] Icons that apply to them. If player has more Skull icons then Hearth Icons, their [[Realignment#Death Event|Death Event]] is triggered. If multiple [[Realignment#Death Event|Death Events]] are triggered at the same time, resolve them in turn order, starting with the player holding the [[Initiative - Tension token]]. If resolving the next [[Realignment#Death Event|Death Event]] would eliminate the last surviving player, don't resolve this event and instead continue with the next step. This means that, if all remaining players would die during the same Survival Step, the last player whose [[Realignment#Death Event|Death Event]] would be resolved survives.
## Hearths and Skulls

Hearth and skull icons can appear on [[Incantation Cards]], [[Domain Boards]] and [[Resonance Cards]]. 

## Death Event

When death event is triggered do the following things:

- Take one of each type of your [[Shard Tokens]] (From [[Game Layout#Player Area|Player area]] and [[Core Tiles]] ) and place them into [[Rupture Board#Calm Zone|Calm Zone]] if the same type of [[Shard Tokens|Shard]] is not there already. Remove the rest of the tokens from the game. The [[Rupture Board#Calm Zone|Calm Zone]] cannot contain multiple [[Shard Tokens]] of the same type.
- Return all [[Rune Tiles]] to the [[Rune Tile Bag]].
- Discard all [[Incantation Cards]] from [[Game Layout#Active zone|Active zone]] and [[Spell#Spell deck|Spell deck]].
- Discard all [[Resonance Cards]]
- Remove the rest of your components from the game. 
- The game ends for you.

# Game End Step

If there is only one player still alive, the game ends And the surviving player is the winner of the game.

# Refresh Step

During this step, next [[Game structure#Weaving|Weaving]] phase is prepared. Do the following in this order: 
- refresh [[Realignment#Rune Tiles Offer refresh|Rune Tiles Offer]]
- refresh [[Realignment#Incantation Offers refresh|Incantation Offers]]
- Turn the [[Initiative - Tension token]] to its [[Initiative - Tension token#Tension side|Tension side]].
## Rune Tiles Offer refresh

Do the following: 
- Remove all the remaining [[Rune Tiles]] from the [[Rune Tiles Offer]] and return them to the [[Rune Tile Bag]].
- Mix the [[Rune Tiles]] in the [[Rune Tile Bag]] draw #TBD tiles at random from the bag and place them in [[Rupture Board#Calm Zone|Calm Zone]].
- Then draw #TBD tiles at random from the bag and place them in designated [[Rupture Board#Ripple Zones|Ripple Zones]]. (One zone = one tile.)
- Finally draw #TBD tiles form the bag and spread them across the [[Rupture Board#Drift zone|Drift zone]].
 
## Incantation Offers refresh

Do the following for each of the [[Domain Boards]]:
- Discard the [[Incantation Cards|Incantation Card]] closest to the [[Rupture Board]] from the offer. 
- Move the remaining [[Incantation Cards]] on space closer to the [[Rupture Board]].
- Draw new [[Incantation Cards|Incantation Card]] and put it on the empty space in the offer.
