# Concept

Resonances are the remains of magical power that stay with the mage long after their entanglement with the [[Domains]] is over. They can be used to access exceptional power, but are dangerously volatile and directly tied to their [[Domains|Domain]] of origin.

Resonances are represented by [[Resonance Cards]] which are two sided. One side contains strong ability (often single use - or limited use) and the other two [[Realignment#Skulls|Skulls]]. The Resonances can be flipped from one side to the other during the game. Most common reasons for the [[Resonance#Fliping|Flip]] is [[Domains#Instability| Domain Instability]] or [[Spell#Collapse of the Core Tiles| Core Tile Collapse]], but can be caused trough other effects as well.  
# Acquiring Resonance

Most common way of acquiring Resonance is trough [[Spell#Release|Spell Release]] or [[Spell#Collapse of the Core Tiles|Spell Core Collapse]]. To acquire the Resonance take two [[Resonance Cards]] form the [[Domain Boards|Domain Board]] and choose one of them. Place the chosen Resonance in the [[Game Layout#Active zone|Active zone]], ability side facing up.    
# Flipping

The [[Resonance Cards]] can be flipped during the game. Flipping means turning the [[Resonance Cards|Resonance Card]] to its other face, regardless of the face it has been facing up before. 
If there are any [[Shard Tokens]] on the [[Resonance Cards|Resonance Card]] at the moment of the flip, remove those tokens from the card, but keep them in the [[Game Layout#Player Area|Player Area]], those can be used again later. 
# Using the Resonance

Most of the abilities on the [[Resonance Cards]] can be activated or used by the player. Usually, the card specifies when it can be used. There are usually one ore more spaces printed on the [[Resonance Cards]] and using the ability requires placing the [[Shard Tokens|Shard Token]] of corresponding [[Domains|Domain]] from [[Game Layout#Player Area|Player Area]] to that designated space. If player does not have enough corresponding [[Shard Tokens]] to place on the [[Resonance Cards|Resonance Card]], the ability cannot be used.
